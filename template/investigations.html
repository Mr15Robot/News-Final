Us against Fake News

360 News has always been against the spread of Fake News. In Order to prevent It, we have a specially built-in system for our writers that won't allow them to upload any Fake News.

We have been fighting the spread of Fake News for over 15 years, and unsurprisingly our work increases each year, since there are more and more attempts at misinformation.

There are three main ways we detect Fake News:

  • Fact Checking
  • Reviewing Supporting Evidence
  • Examining the Writing Style

What we believe

At 360 News, we are driven by key principles that shape our perspective:

  • We prioritize reporting on initiatives and challenges in the pursuit of global peace, offering insights into international relations.
  • Our coverage centers on the values of democracy, emphasizing transparency, accountability, and the protection of individual rights.
  • We confront the menace of Fake News through rigorous fact-checking and reliance on credible sources, aiming to empower our audience with media literacy.

At 360 News, we're dedicated to providing clear and accurate news, helping everyone stay well-informed globally.
